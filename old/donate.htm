MAKE A DONATION

Donations can be made to "MHS Alumni Association" and mailed to:
MHS Alumni Association
P.O. Box 133
Uncasville CT 06382

Donations made to MHSAA are not federally tax deductible. Per our status as 501(c)(7), we are classified as a social organization or club. Presently, we are in the process of becoming 501(c)(3) classified. When this occurs, we will post this information on the website.

You can also help out the MHSAA in other ways. Please consider donating prizes or volunteering your time at any of our events.

  • Montville Fair -- We host a booth and raffle-out a wide variety of GREAT gift baskets. Proceeds to benefit MHSAA Scholarship Fund. Also an opportunity for Alumni to update contact information.
  • Fair Oaks Tag Sales -- proceeds to benefit Montville High School Alumni Association Scholarship Fund
  • MHS Homecoming Game -- We host a booth and raffle-out a wide variety of GREAT gift baskets. Proceeds to benefit MHSAA Scholarship Fund. Also an opportunity for Alumni to update contact information.
  • MHSAA Address Database -- We provide a tool for managing up-to-date contact information of MHS Alumni, used for Reunions and Event Planning
  • Superintendent's Advisory Council (held on 11/18/03, 2/24/04, & 5/5/04) -- MHSAA has provided the Graduation Banner and purchased flowers for the graduates
  • Montville High School Reunions -- Provide assistance and tips on planning Class Reunions
  • Scholarships -- 2 Scholarships are presented annually to graduating Seniors planning to continue his/her education in an accredited two or four year, technical college, university or post-secondary institution. See Details
  • Gifts/Awards -- On a case-by-case basis, we offer gifts to help defray costs of attending leadership seminars or conferences
  • Back to School Night -- MHSAA, with support from Bob's Discount Furniture, helped to furnish the reading rooms. We are continually looking for matching donations/gifts for the organization
  • Marching Madness
  • Social/Dance
