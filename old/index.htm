You have found the official site of the Montville High School Alumni Association. MHSAA is an incorporated entity, answerable to its own Board of Directors, to provide services to its members and the High School.

We hold monthly meetings on the 2nd Monday of the month at 7:00P. Our next meeting is on Tuesday, July 8, 7 p.m. at the Oakdale Firehouse. Please join us!

We welcome visitors to View Entries in the MHSAA Guestbook or Submit an Entry via Email.

If you are a Montville High School graduate, you are automatically a member of MHSAA and are welcome to attend all meetings - we do hope you'll join us! If you wish to become a voting Board Member (have an official say in events, scholarship awards, fundraising, etc), you may pursue that as well. To obtain Board Member status, attend any meeting so that your nomination can be approved through a Board vote. Upon becoming a Board Member, one should maintain an active role in the Board/meetings. Any Board Member who misses two consecutive meetings is at risk to lose Board Member status.

Additionally, or alternatively, one may contribute monetarily by providing Annual Support or purchasing MHSAA merchandise (see below).
