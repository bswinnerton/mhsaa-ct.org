MHSAA SCHOLARSHIP

The first award (MHSAA Scholarship - $500) will be made to a graduating senior of Montville High School (MHS), planning to continue his/her education in an accredited two or four year, technical college, university or post-secondary institution.

The second award (MHS Alumni Parent Scholarship - $750) will be made to a son or daughter of an MHS alumnus, graduating from any accredited high school, planning to continue his/her education in an accredited two or four year, technical college, university or post-secondary institution.

MHSAA SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION

Adobe PDF format: MHSAA 2008 Scholarship Application

The scholarship can be downloaded from the Counseling & Career Scholarship May webpage and from the main C&CNews web page.

HOW TO APPLY

To apply, please print and fill-out the MHSAA Scholarship Application (see above) and mail to:
MHSAA Scholarship
Attn: MHS Alumni Association
P.O. Box 133
Uncasville CT 06382

***All applications must be post marked by May 11, 2007***
