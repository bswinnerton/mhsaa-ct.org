Montville High School Alumni Association Scholarship

The first award (MHSAA Scholarship - $500) will be made to a graduating senior of Montville High School (MHS), planning to continue his/her education in an accredited two or four year, technical college, university or post-secondary institution.

The second award (MHS Alumni Parent Scholarship - $750) will be made to a son or daughter of an MHS alumnus, graduating from any accredited high school, planning to continue his/her education in an accredited two or four year, technical college, university or post-secondary institution.

MHSAA Scholarship Application

Adobe PDF format: MHSAA 2011 Scholarship Application

The scholarship can be downloaded from the Counseling & Career Scholarship May webpage and from the main C&CNews web page.

To apply, please print and fill-out the MHSAA Scholarship Application (see above) and mail to:

How to obtain your scholarship

Congratulations on receiving the MHS Alumni Association, Inc. Scholarship!!

As part of the MHSAA Scholarship award, you are officially recognized as a member of the Alumni Association, Inc. We hope you would encourage your friends to join and participate in the Association. It is a wonderful way to continue to participate in fostering positive attitudes towards your high school. Reunion planning assistance is a large part of this organization in an effort to help with continued post graduation contact.

Your scholarship award will be paid directly to the College/University of your choice in the Spring Semester of your Freshman year.

Please forward the following information to:

MHS Alumni Association Scholarship Fund

Ellen Rosenfeldt, Scholarship Chairperson

P.O. Box 133, Uncasville CT 06382

· Your full name

· Social Security Number

· Name of the Institution you will be attending and their complete mailing address

(Please be sure the address is the correct address to send payments to. i.e.: Bursar, Registrar, etc.)

· Student ID Number and

· a copy of your fall transcript (for verification of enrollment)
